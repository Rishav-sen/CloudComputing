About Us

Welcome to our medicine delivery website, AWS MedExpress designed and developed by Harshang Lad, Nihar, Akshay, Rishav, and Sarvan, postgraduate students of the University of Galway pursing Master's of Science in Information Systesm Management. Our website solves the business problem of providing timely and efficient delivery of medicines to people in need, especially during these difficult times. Our platform connects customers with local pharmacies, providing access to essential medications right from the comfort of their homes. By eliminating the need for in-person visits to the pharmacy, our website helps reduce the spread of communicable diseases and ensures that people get the medicines they need, regardless of their location or mobility. At the same time, we also aim to have a positive social impact by making healthcare more accessible and convenient for everyone. Our team is dedicated to providing a reliable, secure, and user-friendly platform that can help bridge the gap between healthcare providers and patients. We are proud to be based in Galway, a city that is renowned for its vibrant culture, world-class universities, and a strong sense of community. Our website is a testament to our commitment to innovation and our passion for improving the lives of people in our local community and beyond.
